How it's possible

How we train frontier models at a fraction of the cost.

We own every stage — the data, the labelling, the models, and the training loop — so our only cost is compute, and quality compounds.

Own the data at source

Our speech data comes from three sources, all ours: internet audio, speakers we hire and script, and real conversations in the app — all processed on our own machines.

$0 on third-party data — ~85–95% cheaper than buying it

We label it ourselves

We clean and transcribe every hour in-house — several speech-to-text models cross-check each other, and native speakers tag dialect & emotion.

We pay for our own GPUs, not per-minute transcription bills

Small models, built to run cheap

We treat speech like language, so we reuse the whole proven AI toolchain. The models we ship are small and fast to serve.

~7× more conversations per GPU · runs on one small GPU

Train big, ship small

We train large models we never ship — they generate training data and coach a small one. The model we actually serve performs like one 2–3× its size.

Small model ≈ the quality of one 2–3× bigger
