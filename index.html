The mission

Models that talk like a native to the next 5 billion people.

India, Southeast Asia and MENA — the largest voice-first markets on earth, and the ones global labs ignore.

The problem

The world is conversational. Models are not — yet.

Conversation is how humans discover, compare, trust and decide. But software was built around navigation: arrive, search, click, choose.

Today's non-English voice models still don't feel human. The tone is off, latency too high, pacing wrong, cultural cues missing. There's a vast gap from talking to a friend.

The solution

Maya speaks in the dialect, tone and rhythm people grew up with.

It talks like a local, shows things as it talks, and holds a conversation — even when the user doesn't yet know what to ask.

Within the conversation, people discover, decide, shop and act.
